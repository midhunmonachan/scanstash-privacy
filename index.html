Privacy policy

Your receipts stay under your control.

ScanStash helps you scan, search, and export receipts on your Android device. It is designed to work without an account, ads, analytics, cloud sync, or background data upload.

Information ScanStash handles

ScanStash stores and processes the following information locally:

  • Receipt images you capture with the camera or choose with Android Photo Picker.
  • Receipt text recognized by on-device optical character recognition (OCR).
  • Receipt details you enter or edit, including store, date, total, tax, category, and notes.
  • Export files you create, including CSV, PDF, and ZIP files.
  • App settings, including the optional Gallery copy setting.

How information is used

Receipt data is used only to provide the features you request:

  • Save and display receipts in your local receipt library.
  • Recognize receipt text and prefill fields for your review.
  • Search saved receipt details and OCR text.
  • Generate exports when you choose to export.
  • Copy receipt images to Gallery only when you enable that setting.

Local storage and security

Receipt images are stored in app-private storage by default. Receipt details and OCR text are stored in the app's local database. Android app backup is disabled, and ScanStash does not request broad storage access.

Receipt images should not appear in Android Gallery unless you turn on Copy receipts to Gallery. That setting is off by default.

Camera and photo access

ScanStash requests Camera permission only so you can scan receipts. When you import an existing image, Android Photo Picker lets you choose the specific image to provide to the app.

Sharing and exports

ScanStash does not automatically share receipts. When you choose Export, the app creates the formats you selected and lets you save them to a folder or open Android's share sheet. Export files can include receipt details, images, and OCR text. The destination you choose controls what happens to those files afterward.

Network, accounts, ads, and analytics

The current ScanStash release does not:

  • Require or create an account.
  • Display ads or include an advertising SDK.
  • Use analytics or crash-reporting services.
  • Sync receipt data to cloud storage.
  • Declare Android Internet permission.

Retention and deletion

Saved receipts remain on the device until you delete them or uninstall the app. Deleting a receipt removes its saved details, OCR text, thumbnail, and app-private receipt image. Uninstalling ScanStash removes its app-private data.

Copies you previously exported, shared, or placed in Gallery are outside the app's private storage and must be deleted from their destination separately.

Policy changes

This policy will be updated before a ScanStash release adds network access, accounts, analytics, advertising, cloud sync, or materially different data handling. The effective date at the top identifies the current version.
